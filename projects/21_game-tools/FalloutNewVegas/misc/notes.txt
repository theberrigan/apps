- ESM - master file, contains world data
- ESP - plugin file, overrides data of master files (ESM)
- BSA - archive with other assets
- ESMs and ESPs are collections of records
- ESMs and ESPs are usually dependent on other ESMs and ESPs.
  The only exclusion is the root ESM file (FalloutNV.esm)
- ESMs of a given mod is just a list of "parent" ESMs it depends on.

- ESPs always load after ESMs they depend on
- ESMs with newer modified date load after older ESMs
- ESPs with newer modified date load after older ESPs
