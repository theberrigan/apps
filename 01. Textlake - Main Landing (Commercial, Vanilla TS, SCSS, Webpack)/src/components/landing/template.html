    Protect Your Data
    A single document received by the wrong person can cost your company reputation, initiate law suites, and ruin your sales. As a cloud-based solution, TEXTLAKE applies the highest industry standards for privacy and data security.
    Security is the most important part of any application dealing with sensitive information, and therefore Textlake takes it seriously:
    • Textlake is designed by IT professionals who have vast experience in building software for Fortune 500 companies, including financial systems which process over a billion US dollars per year
    • The foundation of security lies in the selection of reliable and verified hosting provider - AWS (Amazon Web Services) - compliant with PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard), ISO 27001 (Security Management Standard), GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and about 40 more additional standards and regulations
    • All sensitive information is encrypted at rest and during transfer on public networks
    • Server-side encryption uses one of the strongest block ciphers available, 256-bit Advanced Encryption Standard (AES-256)
    • HTTPS is enforced for all services using TLS (SSL), including our public website and the dashboard
